Introduction

Benighat Rorang is a rural municipality in Nepal's Bagmati Province that is situated in the Dhading District. 31,475 people were living in the rural municipality, which has a total area of 29.17 square kilometres (11.26 sq mi), as of the 2011 Nepal census.
The Nepalese government reorganised the local level bodies into 753 new local level institutions on March 10, 2017.Benighat Rorang was formed by the merger of the former Mahadevsthan, Benighat, Dhusha, and Jogimara VDCs. Ten wards make up Benighat Rorang, which is designated as the rural municipality's administrative hub.
