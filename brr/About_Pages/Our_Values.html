About

Our values

Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality is deeply rooted in its core values of community, heritage, and sustainability. At the heart of this municipality lies a strong sense of community, where residents prioritize collaboration and mutual support. Traditional Nepalese values of respect for elders and collective responsibility are upheld, fostering a tight-knit bond among inhabitants. This sense of unity transcends cultural and social differences, creating a harmonious environment where everyone feels valued and included.

Preservation of heritage is another fundamental value cherished by the people of Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality. Ancient traditions and customs are revered, passed down from generation to generation as a testament to their cultural identity. Historical landmarks and sacred sites are meticulously maintained, serving as reminders of the municipality's rich heritage and storied past. By honoring their cultural roots, residents of Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality strive to uphold their unique identity and heritage for future generations to cherish.

In alignment with their reverence for nature and tradition, sustainability is a guiding principle for the municipality's development. Conscious efforts are made to protect the pristine environment and natural resources that define the region. From promoting eco-friendly practices to investing in renewable energy initiatives, Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality is committed to preserving its ecological treasures for posterity. By embracing sustainable development practices, they aim to strike a balance between modern progress and environmental conservation, ensuring a legacy of prosperity for generations to come.
