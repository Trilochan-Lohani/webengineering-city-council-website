About

Who we are

Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality, nestled in the heart of Nepal, embodies the essence of rustic charm and natural beauty. Surrounded by lush green hills and adorned with glistening rivers, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. As you traverse through its winding pathways, you'll encounter picturesque landscapes dotted with traditional Nepalese villages, where time seems to stand still. The municipality is home to a diverse community, rich in culture and heritage, where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with modern aspirations.

Adventure enthusiasts will find a plethora of activities to indulge in, from trekking along scenic trails to rafting in the exhilarating waters of the Trishuli River. The rugged terrain of Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality offers ample opportunities for outdoor exploration, including camping under the starlit sky and birdwatching amidst the verdant forests. For those seeking spiritual solace, the municipality boasts several sacred sites and monasteries, where one can immerse themselves in meditation and reflection amidst tranquil surroundings.

In addition to its natural allure, Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality is committed to fostering sustainable development and preserving its ecological treasures. Initiatives promoting eco-tourism and community-based conservation efforts are actively encouraged, ensuring that future generations can continue to marvel at its pristine landscapes. Whether you're an intrepid adventurer, a cultural enthusiast, or simply seeking respite in nature's embrace, Benighat Rorang Rural Municipality welcomes you with open arms, promising an unforgettable experience that will leave a lasting imprint on your soul.
